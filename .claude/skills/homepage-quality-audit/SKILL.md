# Homepage Quality Audit Skill

## Purpose

Audit the portfolio home page as a strategic entry point.

## Checklist

- The hero communicates positioning clearly.
- The user understands Henrico's specialty within 30 seconds.
- The case section creates desire to open the case pages.
- The method section is understandable and not over-technical.
- The impact section reinforces credibility.
- CTAs are clear.
- Visual rhythm is coherent across sections.
- Mobile remains readable and functional.

## Language rule

All homepage content must support both PT-BR and EN-US.

## Output

Report:
- what is working;
- what is weak;
- recommended changes;
- risks;
- whether implementation is required.
