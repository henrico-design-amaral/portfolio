# Anti AI Slop Editorial Review Skill

## Purpose

Remove generic AI-sounding language from portfolio copy.

## Detect and remove

- vague claims;
- buzzwords;
- inflated impact;
- generic UX phrases;
- startup-style hype;
- empty seniority language;
- repeated abstractions without concrete meaning.

## Prefer

- concrete operational language;
- specific decisions;
- constraints;
- trade-offs;
- sober tone;
- technical clarity;
- human readability.

## Language rule

When reviewing portfolio copy, check both PT-BR and EN-US versions.

## Output

Return:
- weak excerpts;
- why they are weak;
- sharper replacement suggestions;
- risk of unsupported claims.
